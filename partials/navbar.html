    It's CHORE Turn!: the Divorce Prophylactic makes staying married easier-ish!
    To use, register your household, create your partner profiles, and enter your household's weekly chores.
    Assign each chore a mutually agreeable number of "Irritation Points."
    As you and your somewhat-loved one complete your chores throughout the week, track them, and Irritation Points will convert to Points Earned.
    Then, when an unexpected chore comes up, we'll delegate it for you using the hard data you've given us, rather than your usual admixture of flawed logic, latent hostility, and bias.
